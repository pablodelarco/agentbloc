## Terms

**Agent**: A specialized AI assistant that performs one specific job in your workflow.

**MCP Server**: A connector that lets AI agents talk to external services (like Google Sheets or Telegram).

**Cron**: An automatic scheduler that runs your agents at specific times (like "every day at 10pm").

**API**: A way for software programs to communicate with each other (like a digital mailbox between services).

**Webhook**: A notification that fires automatically when something happens in a service.

**Pipeline**: A chain of agents where each one passes its output to the next.

**Kill Switch**: An emergency stop button that halts all agent activity immediately.

**Dry Run**: A test run where agents process real data but do not actually send emails, make payments, or modify external services.

# Arco Rooms Property Management

Pablo runs a 30-property rental management business in Almeria, Spain.
Every evening he spends 2-3 hours collecting utility invoices from 6
provider portals, matching bank payments across 4 banks (7 accounts,
PSD2), and messaging owners individually on Telegram. This team
automates the pipeline end-to-end.

## Workflows

| ID | Trigger | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| `cobro-diario` | cron `0 22 * * *` | Daily invoice collection → payment matching → per-owner Telegram summary |
| `unmatched-payment-alert` | inter-agent (3+ unmatched) | Real-time Telegram alert to Pablo |

## Agents

| Agent | Role | Autonomy | Blast |
